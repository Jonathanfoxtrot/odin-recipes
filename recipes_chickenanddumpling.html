Chicken and Dumplings


This warm hearty meal is easy to make and will keep you full for hours.


  • 1 32oz chicken broth
  • 1 can cream of chicken
  • 1/2 pound flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 Tbsp. Italian seasoning
  • 2 Tbsp. chicken seasoning
  • 1 tsp. onion powder
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 chicken breasts

Let's Begin Cooking!

  1. First, start off by bringing the chicken breasts to a boil. Add the Italian, chicken, onion, and garlic powder into the water.

  2. While the chicken is boiling, in another pan, put the chicken broth and cream of chicken together and stir on medium heat

  3. Add the same seasonings used for the chicken breasts to the broth and cream of chicken.

  4. In a bowl add the water and flour to create a dough.

  5. Take large cutting board lightly covered in flour. Roll the dough on the cutting board.

  6. After the dough is rolled. Cut the dough into 1 inch squares drop them into the heated broth.

  7. Take the chicken out of the boiling water. Cut or shred to your liking and add to the broth.

  8. Once dumplings are cooked through, plate, add salt & pepper to taste, and enjoy!
