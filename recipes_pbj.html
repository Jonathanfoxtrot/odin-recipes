It's Peanut Butta Jelly Time!


This intermediate level recipe will have your friends impressed. All of the neighbors and family will be lining up at your door begging you for this recipe.


Ingredients

  • 1 jar jelly(Any jelly of your choice)
  • 1 jar of peanut butter(Crunchy or smooth peanut butter will work)
  • 2 slices of bread

Let's Begin


  1. Open peanut butter and jar containers.

  2. Take out two slices of bread.

  3. Smear peanut butter on one slice of bread and jelly on the other slice

  4. Important step: to the best of your ability, combine both slices together so the peanut butter and jelly come together.

  5. Serve and enjoy!
